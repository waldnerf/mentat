A working vocabulary for enterprise AI.

The concepts that most shape strategy, value, governance and risk, curated for the C-suite. Sized by importance, grouped into five families, and generated from one source so it stays current.

The language of AI changes quickly. Terms that were unfamiliar a year ago now sit in budgets, board papers, and regulation. When the vocabulary drifts, so does the ability to ask a precise question, test a vendor’s claim, or tell a real capability from a relabelled one. Keeping the words current is how leaders hold authority over decisions increasingly framed in them. The most important terms sit in the middle; read outward.
