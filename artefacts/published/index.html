About

A way of thinking, not a brand around technology

Mentat optimises for clarity over volume, systems over isolated outputs, and reuse over reinvention. The canonical Markdown knowledge asset is the source of truth. Derived outputs, from websites to presentations to talks, are generated from that source and regenerated as it improves.
